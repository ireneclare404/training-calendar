# training-calendar
A fully personalizable training calendar, every effort counts, stick it on.

**Why this exists**
Most training apps decide for you what counts as exercise. This one doesn't. You define the categories, the intensities, and the colors — no bending your life to fit someone else's training menu. The effort that appears on no menu at all, but that you value, deserves a sticker too.

**How it works**

**Points.** Everything converts to one simple scale: 1 point = 1 minute lying at rest. A 10x category earns 10 points per minute. The score is saved the moment you log an entry, so changing your settings later never rewrites your history.

**What points are.** A personal comparison tool — not calories, not a medical measurement. They let very different activities sit on one calendar.

**Everyone is different.** The same move costs different people different effort, so the reference tables give ranges instead of single numbers. Pick within the range by how hard it honestly feels for you.

**Categories.** Each has a name, a multiplier, and a color — all editable in Personalize. If one session mixes intensities, make two categories and log two entries.

**Stickers.** Entries of the same color on one day merge into one dot, and the dot's area matches its points: small effort, small dot; big day, big dot. Hover or tap a dot to see what's inside.

**Bodyweight.** Rep-based moves are scored per rep, each with its own editable value. Defaults are calibrated so a full session (10 pull-ups, 50 push-ups, 50 jump squats, 100 sit-ups) = 300 points — the same as 30 minutes at 10x.

**Your data.** Everything stays in your own browser; nothing is uploaded. CSV exports are for analysis; the complete backup saves records and settings together and can be imported back anytime.
